Japan

Welcome To Japan

Japan is an island located in East Asia in the Pacific Ocean. The region is renowned for its mountainous landscapes, mostly covered by forests. Most areas have a temperate climate and experience four distinct seasons. Japan is rich in history and boasts a unique culture, evident in its traditional structures and the tools and weapons created in the region. From ancient structures still in use to the modern capsule hotels Japan is famous for, each region offers plenty to explore and experience. It's a fantastic destination for those interested in both modern and traditional cultures and their enduring relevance today.
