Greece

Welcome To Greece

Greece, a region of Europe with one of the longest coastlines, is known for its beaches, ancient ruins, and its significant influence on the world. Some of the myths, monsters, and heroes of this region have inspired various video games. The region comprises numerous islands scattered around the Aegean Sea, providing diverse settings for games and offering ample areas to explore. Greece not only offers a hot Mediterranean climate and beautiful beaches but also allows visitors to explore ancient ruins. Sampling the local food and culture makes it a destination suitable for a wide range of interests beyond just exploration.

  • Immortals Fenyx Rising
